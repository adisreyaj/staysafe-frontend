Overview

What is Corona Virus?

Coronaviruses are a huge group of infections which may cause sickness in animals or people. In people, a few coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections running from the common cold to increasingly serious infections, for example, Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The most as of late found coronavirus causes coronavirus illness COVID-19.

What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is the infectious disease brought about by the most as of late found coronavirus. This new infection and ailment were unknown before the outbreak started in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Treatment

There is no particular medication to prevent or treat coronavirus disease (COVID-19). People may require medical care to help them to overcome.
