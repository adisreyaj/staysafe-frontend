Symptoms

The most well-known side effects of COVID-19 are fever, tiredness, and dry cough. A few patients may have a throbbing painfulness, nasal clog, runny nose, sore throat or diarrhea. These signs are normally gentle and start gradually. A few people become infected however don't build up any sign and don't feel unwell. The vast majority (about 80%) recover from the disease without requiring exceptional treatment. More established individuals, and those with basic medical issues like high blood pressure, heart problems or diabetes, are more likely to develop serious illness. People with fever, cough and difficulty breathing should seek medical attention.
